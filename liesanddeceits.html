Lies and Deceits

Your collective display of cowardice has left me astounded; it is evident that you never possessed the faintest inkling of what you were truly doing. Such ignorance and timidity are truly disheartening. Reflecting upon the grand year of '92, I cannot help but recall how you all, without hesitation, abandoned me. The memory remains vivid, a poignant reminder of your lack of loyalty and courage. The ramifications of your actions continue to reverberate, leaving a lasting mark on my existence. Nevertheless, I persevere, driven by the knowledge that true strength lies not in the companionship of cowards, but in the resilience of an indomitable spirit.
