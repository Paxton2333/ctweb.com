CT Information

CT was established in 2016 by Paxton as an elite organization dedicated to defending humanity against evil aliens and malevolent groups.

With over 1,000,000 members across the universe, CT stands as a beacon of hope, ready to confront any impending peril.

One of CT's notable assets is its powerful destruction weapon, developed for emergency situations, ensuring the safety of Earth and its inhabitants.

CT's members are highly trained professionals from various fields, capable of operating seamlessly in high-pressure situations.

Throughout its existence, CT has successfully thwarted numerous invasions and acts of aggression from hostile forces.

Beyond its defensive operations, CT actively engages in interstellar diplomacy, forging alliances with other peacekeeping organizations and advanced civilizations.

CT stands as a beacon of strength and resilience, ready to face the challenges that arise from the vast reaches of the universe.
